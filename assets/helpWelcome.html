Welcome to SoccerStats!

Touch the Teams, Players, & Games button to access information and statistics on your team and its associated players and games. Initially you'll see your team's name. You can edit information about your team by touching the team's name until the edit prompts are shown (long-tap).

A quick touch-and-release (short-tap) on the team's name will bring up the Players and Games tabs.

  1. The Players tab allows you to see and modify player information. With a long-tap you can edit the selected player's details. You can add new players by touching the Menu button and selecting Add player.

  1. The Games tab allows you to see summary and detail information regarding the games played by your team. Game details are available by touching the game you're interested in.

Going back to the main screen, click on the Start Game! button to start recording game and player statistics. Once in the game play screen, clicking on the Menu button provides additional options such as editing game information (e.g., opposing team name) and adding new players. During game play the Menu button also supports “undo” capability. This is useful in case you make a mistake recording a game event such as choosing the wrong team when a goal is scored. You can also “undo” mistakes by a long-tap on the game button representing the action you want to undo (e.g., Score).

The tabs above provide more information to help get you started on your way to successfully using SoccerStats.
